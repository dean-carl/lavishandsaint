About Me

My name is Georgina and I offer private travel concierge services. I have a huge passion for travel, and can help you travel smarter, and travel in style. From exotic vacation packages to private jets, chartered yachts to sporting events, for either small groups or large corporate bookings. I am here to help you plan, co-ordinate and book all your luxury travel needs and desires.

A Television Producer in a previous life, I have travelled the globe and been to an array of continents and countries. Working on various programmes from glamorous awards ceremonies, to well known property and travels shows.

Leaving the world of television, I wanted to follow my travel dreams and retrained as a fully qualified independent travel consultant, launching my very own travel company; Lavish and Saint Travel.
